HR Clan Guide

A complete guide for HRs on handling tickets, events, and blacklists

Verification Tickets

  • Accept the player into the group and update them with Rover.
  • If they aren't verified, instruct them to verify with Rover.
  • Add the correct timezone role:
    • EST – Americas
    • GMT – Europe, Africa, Middle East
    • Asia – Asia & Oceania
  • Save the ticket transcript before closing and deleting it.
  • Note: CW cases are not accepted.

Raid Request Tickets

  • Give the player the raid request role.
  • Do not delete or close the ticket.

Link:
  • Take 1 screenshot of player list before event ends.
  • Take a victory screenshot after event:
    • If lost, only player list screenshot needed
    • Post victory screenshots in #victories with topfrag & secondfrag MVPs
  • Distribute XP via /add xp in #rankings:
    • 15 XP – top 2 frags in events
    • 10 XP – all other players in events
    • 5 XP – spectators (subs)
    • 5 XP – each player in DTS
    • HRs & elites cannot receive XP
  • Elite logs: track topfrag/secondfrag over total events, promote after 5 top/second frags.
  • Active & knowledgeable HRs will be promoted automatically.
